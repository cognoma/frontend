Welcome to Cognoma

putting machine learning in the hands of cancer biologists

Use Cognoma to predict cancer mutations from gene expression profiles, based on public TCGA data.

Start by selecting which mutations and cancer types to model. Based on your selection, Cognoma will run the analysis and email you a Jupyter notebook with the results.

Cognoma is an open source tool created by the Philadelphia community, including the DataPhilly Meetup, the Childhood Cancer Data Lab at Alex's Lemonade Stand Foundation, and the Greene Lab at Penn. Learn more or ask questions on the project's GitHub.
